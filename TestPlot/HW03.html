This is a Visualization of a computational fluid flow simulation dataset from the San Diego Supercomputing Center.

Use the slider to move the plane through the data, which will then plot the (x,y) position of those particles at that z value. The concentration goes from 0 - ~360, colored as white - black, or white - dark blue if inside the plane.

L-Mouse rotates the camera, R-Mouse pans the camera, Mid-Mouse does zoom
